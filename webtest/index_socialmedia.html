Social Media: Twitter Criticism Analysis

Twitter is a popular social media tools. A popular twitter's tweets are usually re-tweeted by follower. However, if one of the popular twitter users always publishes the negative message, this user will have a high probability to produce the negative effects on society.

  • We find these popular twitter users, whose twitter account is with more than 500K follower, and find the top popular users who always publish the negative message.
  • We try to use the result as our evidence to persuade them to decrease their published negative message numbers, such as sending messages to them with the analysis result and potential negative effects for the society.
