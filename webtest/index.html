About Project

Big data analysis is becoming more and more popular currently. Big data not just means the amount of data is large, but also means the types of data is various. Big data analysis can benefit human life in many ways.

In this project, with the help of Nectar Cloud Resource, our aim is to harvest tweets which are sent from New York on Twitter, and perform some data analysis on these data in order to study New York in different respects e.g. entertainment, sport, health, culture etc. Technologies listed on the right side are implemented in this projects.

Technologies

The all task is divided into 4 stages: Configuring cloud environment, Harvesting Tweets from New York, Analying data from different perspectives, and presenting results in webpages.
